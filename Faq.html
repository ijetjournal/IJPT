ISAR IJPT - Frequently Asked Questions:

    Q . How to submit my paper?

    You have to submit your paper through online submission link submit your paper.


    Q .How much it takes for review process?

    The International Journal of Engineering & Science takes 3 - 5 days for publication of paper.


    Q .When I have to deposit fees?

    You have to deposit fees only after getting acceptance letter.


    Q .What are publication charges?

    Publication charges are 1000 INR or 61(USD).


    Q .How to pay fees?

    1.You can pay through Online using credit/Debit card/ netbanking /cash card/ etc.
    2.By directly depositing in IJIRAE bank account holder through nearby bank branch.
    3.By transferring fees through their net banking option.
    4.For more clarification feel free to mail us at IJPTchief@gmail.com.


    Q .Do I need to submit the copyright form along with the manuscript?

    No, copyright forms shall be accepted by ISAR IJPT only if the submission is accepted for publication..


    Q .Where can I find Copyrights Transfer Form?

    Kindly refer to Author Instructions link. If your paper is selected, download the Copyright Form from the author instruction page and send it with the final version of paper.
